Honey Mustard Chicken and Rice

Summary

The honey-mustard chicken dish (henceforth HMC) is a staple from my childhood. Although I do endeavour to avoid the pitfalls of modern recipe websites (namely, lameting and reminiscing about childhood meals), it does get the #1 spot for it's person significance.

It is an a simple chicken and rice dish, oven-baked, with a sauce combining the delights of ... honey and mustard. Complete it with some rice and vegetables, and you have a nourishing tasty treat.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Honey
  • Mustard

Method

  1. Prepare the sauce.
  2. Bake the chicken in the sauce.
  3. Serve with the rice.
