Water

Summary

Water is the life-blood that fuels the dreams of champions.

We should all drink more of it. So light on the fizz that you can slam it down hard.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Ice (Optional)
  • Steam (Optional)

Method

  1. If you've got water, you're done.
  2. If you've got ice, you'll need to wait a bit.
  3. If you've got steam, you'll need to trap and cool it! Good luck!
