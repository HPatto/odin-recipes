Risotto

Summary

Risotto was a perfect combination of warm, hearty, and simple. It kept my cold heart warm through the brief but striking English winters we experienced.

Typically a combination of rice, vegetables, and spices, it is highly customizable, and can be adapted to include spices and meats.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Egg
  • Spring onions
  • Tumeric

Method

  1. Put all the ingredients in a stir-fry pan.
  2. Smash 'em up and let them simmer.
  3. Serve with gusto.
